About Us

Dramaturgy Theatre Group is a premier destination for theatre enthusiasts in Laxmi Nagar, Delhi. Established in 2014, the company offers a diverse range of enriching Acting Classes activities for individuals of all ages. With passionate instructors who are experts in their fields, Dramaturgy Theatre Group prides itself on providing a supportive and friendly learning environment that encourages creativity and exploration.
